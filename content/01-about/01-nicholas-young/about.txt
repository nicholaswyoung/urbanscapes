text:
Over a decade ago in rural Alabama, Nicholas set the aperture, focused the lens, and pressed the shutter button, creating the first in a long line of striking images. Today, he works as a multi-discliplinary creative, focusing on film, photography, and audio production at his company, (link: http://originalmachine.com text: Original Machine).

After moving to Chicago in 2010, he set out with open eyes to create the work you see here. "_Urbanscapes_ isn't just a collection of photos, it's a redefinition of how we should view our city. Even the dirt can be beautiful."
